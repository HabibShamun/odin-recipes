Crispy Fried Chicken

Document

Description:

Crispy fried chicken is a popular dish consisting of chicken pieces coated in a seasoned batter or breading and deep-fried until golden brown and crispy on the outside while remaining juicy and tender on the inside.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken pieces (drumsticks, thighs, breasts, wings)
  • Salt and pepper
  • All-purpose flour
  • Egg
  • Breadcrumbs or cornflakes crumbs
  • Seasonings (paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, cayenne pepper - optional)
  • Oil for frying

Steps:

Prepare the chicken:
  • Season the chicken pieces with salt and pepper.
Coat the chicken:
  • Dredge each piece of chicken in flour, then dip it in beaten eggs, and finally coat it in breadcrumbs or cornflakes crumbs mixed with seasonings.
Set up the frying:
  • Heat oil in a deep fryer or a large pot to around 350-375째F (175-190째C).
Fry the chicken:
  • Carefully place the coated chicken pieces in the hot oil, a few at a time, making sure not to overcrowd the pan.
  • Fry the chicken for about 15-20 minutes, turning occasionally, until they are golden brown and cooked through. The internal temperature should reach 165째F (74째C).
Drain and rest:
  • Remove the fried chicken from the oil and place them on a wire rack or paper towels to drain excess oil.
  • Let the chicken rest for a few minutes before serving.
Serve and enjoy:
  • Serve the crispy fried chicken hot with your favorite sides like mashed potatoes, coleslaw, or cornbread.
  • Enjoy the crispy, flavorful goodness of homemade fried chicken!
